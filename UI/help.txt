interacting with the timeline and canvas:

hold left mouse button to move the view, scroll to zoom in and out
click with left mouse button to select a dot

press R: switch recording on/off, default is off
press Space: switch timeflow on/off, default is off
press F: forget the selected dot
press H: hide the selected dot
press L: switch labels ont he canvas on/off, default is off

When recording is set on:

hold right mouse button to move the dot around

press B + click right mouse button on an unselected dot to
bind the selected dot to it

press B + click right mouse button on the selected dot to
delete the current bind



Interacting with damperprofile painter:

hold left mouse button to bloat the shape
hold right mouse button to deflate the shape
